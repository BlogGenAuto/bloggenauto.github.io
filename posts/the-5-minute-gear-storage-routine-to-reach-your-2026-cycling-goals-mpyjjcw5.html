The 5-Minute Post-Ride Gear Storage Routine

Consistency is key to reaching your 2026 cycling goals. This 5-minute routine, powered by your heavy duty hanging hooks in garage, ensures your bike is ready to roll at a moment's notice.

  1. Minute 1: Secure the Bike. Roll the bike into your no-lift bike storage hook. If it's an e-bike, ensure it's clicked into place securely.
  2. Minute 2: Wipe Down. Use a quick cloth to wipe the chain and frame. This is easier when the bike is held vertically at chest height.
  3. Minute 3: Hang the Essentials. Place your helmet, backpack, and sweaty shoes on the secondary wall mounted gear hook.
  4. Minute 4: Tech Check. Remove your GPS and lights and connect them to the nearby charging cables.
  5. Minute 5: Prep the Next Ride. Check your Strava route for tomorrow and lay out your nutrition.
