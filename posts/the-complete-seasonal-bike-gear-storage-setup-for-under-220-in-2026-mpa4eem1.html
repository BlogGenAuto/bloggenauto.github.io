Frequently Asked Questions

Are garage hooks worth it?

Garage hooks are simple storage tools that let you hang items on walls or ceilings to save space and stay organized. They are absolutely worth the investment because they prevent gear damage and reclaim valuable floor space. You can use them to store tools, bikes, ladders, and sports gear safely, ensuring your garage remains a functional workspace rather than a storage locker.

How to hang stuff in your garage?

Using rail-based systems or pegboards is an easy way to incorporate hanging storage in your garage for items of all weights. For heavy items like bicycles, using a dedicated rail system with garage hooks for storage ensures the load is distributed across multiple wall fixings. This solution is great for creating massive storage walls or keeping essential maintenance tools organized over a workbench.

What are the different types of garage hooks?

There are several varieties designed for specific needs: Double Hooks for stability, Ladder Hooks for long bulky items, and Utility Hooks for general gear. For cyclists, Bicycle Storage Hooks with rubber sleeves are essential. Heavy Duty Garage Hooks are specifically engineered to hold higher weights like e-bikes, while Tool-Specific hooks keep your workshop tidy. There are also overhead and sports-specific variants.

How much weight can garage hooks hold?

The weight capacity varies significantly by design. While standard single hooks might support 15-20kg, heavy-duty cradle hooks can support up to 90kg (200 lbs) when properly anchored. The GearHooks® system supports up to 20kg per individual hook, which is ideal for most 2026 mountain bikes and e-bikes.
