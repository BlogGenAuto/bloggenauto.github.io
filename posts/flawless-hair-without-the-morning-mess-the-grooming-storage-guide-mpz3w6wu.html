Yes, sodium hyaluronate is a gentle, biocompatible ingredient. In our hydrating hair pomade, it serves to draw moisture into the hair cuticle, making it beneficial for curly, straight, fine, or thick hair alike, without causing irritation.

How often should I clean my grooming storage area?
