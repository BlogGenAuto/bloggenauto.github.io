Frequently Asked Questions

How much do custom wedding suits cost?

The cost of custom wedding suits typically ranges from $400 to $2,000 depending on fabric quality and construction. High-end online options provide a luxury experience at a mid-range price point by reducing overhead costs while maintaining premium wool blends and half-canvas builds.

What is the difference between a $500 suit and a $5000 suit?

A $5000 suit usually involves full-canvas construction, exotic fabrics like vicuña, and hundreds of hours of hand-stitching in a luxury house. A high-quality $500 custom suit focuses on essential durability, machine-assisted precision, and standard premium wools, offering 90% of the luxury at 10% of the cost.

What is the difference between a $100 suit and a $1000 suit?

A $100 suit is typically made of synthetic polyester, which lacks breathability and sheen, and uses glued interlinings that bubble over time. A $1000 suit utilizes natural fibers, professional tailoring techniques, and tailored silhouettes that enhance the wearer's physique and last for years.
