Frequently Asked Questions

How to hang stuff in your garage?

The most effective way to hang gear in your garage is by using a modular rail system. This allows you to slide hooks for garage organizer into different positions as your equipment collection grows. For heavy items like road bikes, ensure you are anchoring the rail into solid brick, concrete, or wooden studs using high-quality fixings.

What items should not be stored in a garage?

Items sensitive to extreme temperature fluctuations, such as carbon fibre bike frames in uninsulated sheds, require care. However, with a high-quality wall mounted bike rack, you can store your bike indoors in a climate-controlled office or hallway. Avoid storing damp cycling clothing in a garage without ventilation, as this can lead to mold and degradation of technical fabrics.

What is the best way to organize your garage?

The best organization strategy is to clear the floor and utilize wall height. Group related items together—for example, a "Cycling Hub" containing your bike, track pump, and helmet on a single GearRail™. By using specific hooks for garage organizer rails, you create a "home" for every piece of gear, making it easier to maintain an tidy space.
