Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight can a bike rack wall garage support?

A high-quality, UK-made bike rack wall garage using heavy-duty fixings can support up to 100kg across the rail. This makes it suitable for a mix of heavy e-bikes and lightweight carbon road bikes, provided the wall it is attached to is structurally sound.

What is the 2026 recommendation for cycling nutrition?

In 2026, the focus is on personalized fueling. This involves consuming between 60g and 90g of carbohydrates per hour during high-intensity efforts, and ensuring a precise 4:1 carb-to-protein ratio during the immediate recovery window.

Do vertical bike racks damage the wheels?

No, professional vertical bike racks use soft rubber-coated hooks to protect the rims. The weight of the bike is distributed through the hook, which is much less pressure than the force exerted on the wheel during a standard ride over rough terrain.

Can I fit 3 mountain bikes on a 1-metre rail?

Yes, by adjusting the height of the hooks or angling the handlebars, you can comfortably fit 3 mountain bikes. The 1-metre rail is designed to provide enough clearance for the wider handlebars common in 2026 mountain bike geometry.
