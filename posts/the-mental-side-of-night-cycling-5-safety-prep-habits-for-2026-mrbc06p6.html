5 Critical Night Safety Prep Habits for 2026

Developing a routine is the best way to stay safe. Use your wall hooks as the anchor for these five habits:

  1. The 360-Degree Wipe Down: Use the eye-level position to wipe the frame. This isn't just for looks; it helps you feel for cracks or loose components that are hard to see in low light.
  2. Lumens Audit: Switch on your lights while the bike is on the best wall bike mount to verify battery levels.
  3. Drivetrain Lubrication: Use the hooks as a simple work stand to spin the pedals and ensure your chain is silent and efficient.
  4. Brake Pad Clearance: Check that your pads haven't worn down to the indicators, a vital check for the wet 2026 autumn season.
  5. Gear Stowage: Store your high-vis vest and helmet on the handlebars while the bike is mounted so you never forget them.
