Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my pocket comb?

For optimal hygiene and to maintain the anti-static material performance, you should rinse your comb at least once a week. Use warm water and a mild soap to remove any product buildup. This ensures you aren't transferring old pomade or oils back onto your clean hair.

Is an oil-infused comb greasy to the touch?

No, the oil is infused into the material at a molecular level. It doesn't leave a residue on your hands or make your hair feel heavy. Instead, it subtly helps distribute the natural oils already present on your scalp, enhancing shine and health over time.

Why is nail care for men linked to using a pocket comb?

In the professional world of 2026, grooming is about total presentation. A pocket comb represents a commitment to detail. If you care enough to keep your hair in place, you should care enough to keep your nails clean and trimmed. The two together form the basis of modern professional etiquette.

Can I use this comb on a wet beard?
