Keeping a road bike in pristine condition requires regular maintenance, which can be hampered by disorganized spaces. Clutter not only makes it difficult to find tools but also increases the risk of damaging your bike during repairs.

The Frustration of Limited Space

Many cyclists store their bikes in garages or small apartments, where space is at a premium. This often leads to cramped conditions that make maintenance tasks cumbersome and less efficient.
