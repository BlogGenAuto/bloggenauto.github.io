Frequently Asked Questions

Is a wall bike rack vertical safe for carbon rims?

Yes, provided the hook is properly cushioned. High-quality vertical racks use soft PVC or rubber sleeves to distribute the weight and prevent any scratching or pressure points on the rim surface. This is a standard safety feature for 2026 storage solutions.

How much weight can a heavy-duty steel bike hook hold?

Industrial-grade steel hooks, like those used in the GearHooks system, are designed to hold over 25kg when properly mounted. This makes them more than capable of supporting modern e-bikes, which are common in the 2026 commuting landscape.

Can I install a wall bike rack vertical in a small apartment hallway?

Absolutely. The vertical orientation is specifically designed to minimize the bike's protrusion into the room. By hanging the bike flat against the wall or at a slight angle, you can keep hallways clear and functional.

Do I need to drill new holes every time I add a hook?

No, not with a rail-based modular system. Once the main GearRail™ is installed, additional hooks for backpacks, helmets, or spare wheels can be added or moved along the rail in seconds using a simple hex key, requiring no further drilling into your walls.

Why should I choose galvanised steel over plastic storage?

Galvanised steel offers superior longevity and environmental benefits. Unlike plastic, which can become brittle and snap over time, steel provides a lifetime of service. In 2026, choosing durable materials is a key part of sustainable consumer practice.
