Frequently Asked Questions

Can noise-cancelling headphones relieve tinnitus?

While noise cancelling headphones and earbuds are not a medical cure for tinnitus, they can help manage the symptoms. By reducing background noise, they allow users to listen to soothing sounds or white noise at lower, safer volumes. According to the Mayo Clinic, managing environmental sound levels is a common strategy for those living with tinnitus.

Can noise cancelling cause dizziness?

For a small percentage of users, the pressure changes created by Active Noise Cancellation can cause mild dizziness or "ear pressure." This usually occurs when the ANC is exceptionally strong. If you experience this, try using the earbuds in "Transparency Mode" or lower the ANC intensity via the smart touch screen settings.

What are the best noise-cancelling headphones earbuds?

The best noise-cancelling headphones and earbuds in 2026 are those that offer a balance of ANC depth, low latency, and battery life. What headphones or earbuds have the best noise cancelling?
