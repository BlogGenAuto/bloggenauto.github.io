Frequently Asked Questions

Is a vertical bike storage rack wall mounted safe for carbon rims?

Yes, provided the hooks are designed correctly. The BR6FV features soft rubber sleeves that protect the finish of both carbon and alloy rims. Because the bike's weight is distributed vertically through the wheel, it mimics the stresses of riding and does not damage the rim structure.

Can I fit different sizes of bikes on the same rail?

Absolutely. The bike storage rack wall mounted is designed to be modular. You can mix adult mountain bikes, road bikes, and even smaller children's bikes on the same system by adjusting the hook height and horizontal position along the rail.

How difficult is the installation process for the GearHooks BR6FV?

Installation is straightforward and designed for DIY enthusiasts. Each 1-meter rail requires only three holes. The system comes with heavy-duty hex bolts and nylon wall plugs suitable for brick, stone, or concrete. An Allen key is used to position the hooks precisely where you need them.

Will this rack hold my 29er mountain bike with 3-inch tires?

Yes. The hooks are designed to accommodate tire widths up to 3 inches, making them compatible with almost all modern mountain bikes and "plus" size tires popular in 2026.

Do I need to lift my heavy e-bike onto the rack?

No. By positioning heavy bikes at the ends of the rack, you can utilize the "roll-on" technique. Simply apply the back brake, step back to bring the bike onto its rear wheel, and roll it forward until the front wheel catches the hook. No heavy lifting is required.
