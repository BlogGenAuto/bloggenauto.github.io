Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best facial routine for dry skin?

Choose cleansers that are gentle and fragrance-free to avoid stripping natural oils. Avoid long, hot showers which can further dehydrate the face. Treat your dry skin gently and avoid harsh scrubbing or rubbing. Most importantly, apply a high-quality moisturizing emulsion regularly to help relieve dry skin and lock in moisture throughout the day.

What is the best facial for extremely dry skin?

A Hydra Facial is often considered the best overall for extremely dry skin. In contrast to harsh extractions, this procedure is mild and deeply hydrates the skin. The effect is that your skin appears to be full, shiny, and glowing right away because the process cleanses while simultaneously infusing the skin with lots of moisture and antioxidants.

What is the 4 2 4 rule in skincare?

The 4-2-4 rule is a cleansing method that focuses on thorough yet gentle skin care. It involves massaging an oil cleanser for 4 minutes to break down sebum, followed by a water-based cleanser for 2 minutes, and finishing with a 4-minute rinse using lukewarm water. This ensures all impurities are removed without damaging the moisture barrier.

What do dermatologists recommend for dry facial skin?

Dermatologists often recommend using an ointment or cream rather than a lotion for intense hydration. Ingredients like shea butter, lactic acid, urea, hyaluronic acid, and glycerin are essential. Additionally, grape seed extract is highly recommended for its antioxidant properties that protect dry skin from environmental stressors while improving overall texture.
