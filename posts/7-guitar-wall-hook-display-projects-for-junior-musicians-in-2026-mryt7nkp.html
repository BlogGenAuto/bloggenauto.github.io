FAQ Section

Is it okay to hang guitars on the wall?

Yes, hanging guitars on the wall is highly recommended for both storage and display. It keeps instruments away from floor-level hazards like pets, vacuum cleaners, and young children, while providing a stable environment. When using high-quality hooks with silicone protection, your guitar's finish and neck integrity are perfectly preserved.

What is the best wall hook for a guitar?

The best wall hook for a guitar is one that offers a combination of heavy-duty construction, non-reactive padding (like silicone), and adjustability. Rail-based systems like GearHooks are superior because they allow you to store multiple instruments in a compact space and can be adjusted as your collection grows or changes.

What is the best way to display guitars on the wall?

The best way to display guitars is using a vertical or slightly angled wall rack. This maximizes wall space and creates a visual impact. For junior musicians, a 'growth chart' style display that shows instruments of different sizes in chronological order of learning is both practical and inspiring.

How to hang a guitar from a wall?

To hang a guitar correctly, you should first identify the studs in your wall for a secure anchor. Install a high-quality rail or single mount using heavy-duty fixings. Ensure the hook prongs are spaced correctly for the guitar's nut width and that they are covered in a protective material like silicone to prevent damage to the neck.
