Frequently Asked Questions

How long do lash clusters stay on?

With proper application using the lash bond and seal glue, clusters typically stay on for 3 to 7 days. This depends on your skin type (oily vs. dry) and how well you avoid steam and oil-based cleansers during the first 24 hours of wear.

Can I shower or swim with these DIY lash extensions?

Yes! Once the seal has fully cured, the lash bond and seal glue is water-resistant. However, for maximum longevity, we recommend avoiding direct, high-pressure water on the lashes and patting them dry gently after washing your face.

Are lash clusters better than strip lashes?

Most 2026 beauty enthusiasts prefer lash clusters because they sit underneath the natural lashes, making them virtually invisible. They are also much more comfortable as they allow your eyelid to move naturally without the stiff "band" of a strip lash.

Is the DIY lash extension kit safe for sensitive eyes?

Our formula is designed to be low-irritation and fume-free. However, we always recommend doing a patch test on a small section of lashes or your skin before full application to ensure you have no sensitivities to the bond.

How many times can I reuse the clusters?

If handled carefully with lash tweezers and cleaned properly with the remover, each cluster can be reused 3-5 times. This makes the 148-piece kit an incredibly sustainable and cost-effective choice.
