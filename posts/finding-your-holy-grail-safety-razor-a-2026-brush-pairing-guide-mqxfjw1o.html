Frequently Asked Questions

Is an adjustable safety razor good for beginners?

Yes, it is arguably the best choice for beginners. Because you can set the razor to a 'mild' setting (Level 1), it is much more forgiving as you learn the proper angle and pressure required for wet shaving.

How often should I change the Solingen blades?

Most men find that a high-quality Solingen blade lasts for 5 to 7 shaves. However, this depends on the thickness of your beard and how well you maintain the blade between uses.

Can I use any shaving brush with this razor?

Absolutely. While synthetic brushes are the 2026 favorite for their hygiene and ease of use, badger and boar hair brushes work perfectly well with an adjustable brass razor to create a rich lather.

What is the benefit of a brass razor over stainless steel?

Brass is naturally resistant to bacteria and offers a unique weight and balance that many find superior for a gravity-assisted shave. It also develops a beautiful patina over time or can be polished to stay like new.

How do I clean my butterfly open razor?

Simply open the butterfly head, rinse with warm water after each use to remove soap scum, and leave it open to air dry. For a deep clean, a soft toothbrush and mild soap once a month will keep the brass shining.
