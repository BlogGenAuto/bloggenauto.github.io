Decoding the Stroker: Material Safety and Porosity

One of the most critical factors in choosing a stroker is the material it is crafted from. In the budget tier, many products utilize Thermoplastic Rubber (TPR) or TPE. While these can feel soft and lifelike, they are often porous. Porous materials contain microscopic holes that can trap bacteria, skin cells, and fluids, making them difficult to fully sanitize over time.

The Risks of Phthalates in Low-Cost Gear

Budget devices sometimes bypass rigorous testing, potentially containing phthalates used to soften plastics. Porosity and Material Degradation
