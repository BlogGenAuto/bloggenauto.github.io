How to Apply Hair Cream Pomade for Maximum Volume

Even the best hair cream pomade requires the correct technique to shine. To get the most out of your beeswax hair wax, follow these professional steps:

  1. Start with Clean, Damp Hair: Applying to slightly damp hair allows the product to spread more evenly.
  2. Emulsify: Take a nickel-sized amount and rub it between your palms until it turns clear. The heat from your hands activates the three butters.
  3. Root to Tip: Apply starting from the back of the head to the front, focusing on the roots for maximum lift.
  4. Style and Set: Use your fingers for a textured, natural hair styling look, or a comb for a more structured finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is hair cream pomade better than gel?

Yes, for most styles, cream pomade is superior because it doesn't contain alcohol, which dries out the hair and scalp. Pomade offers a flexible hold that can be restyled, whereas gel hardens and can flake throughout the day.

Can I use beeswax hair wax every day?

Absolutely. High-quality beeswax hair wax, especially when blended with shea and camellia butters, provides nourishment. Since it is water-soluble, it won't build up, making it perfectly safe for daily use in a 2026 grooming routine.
