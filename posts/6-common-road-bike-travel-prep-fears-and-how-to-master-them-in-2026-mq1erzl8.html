How does a gear rail help with travel prep?

A gear rail acts as a central hub. It allows you to store your bike alongside your track pump, helmet, and toolkit. This visual consolidation means you can check all your items at once before loading the car, significantly reducing the chance of leaving vital gear behind.

Is horizontal storage better than vertical for road bikes?

Horizontal storage is often preferred for road bikes with flat top tubes as it allows you to use the rack as a simple work stand. It also keeps the bike's profile closer to the wall in narrow garages, though vertical storage is better if you are trying to fit 5 or 6 bikes in a small space.
