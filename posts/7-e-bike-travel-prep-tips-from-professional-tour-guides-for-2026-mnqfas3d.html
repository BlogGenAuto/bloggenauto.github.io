Frequently Asked Questions

Can you hang an ebike on a wall?

Lifting an ebike onto a hook mounted high on the wall, with the back wheel off the floor, is possible but ebikes are very heavy. You need to be careful that you don't hurt your back when lifting the bike and while trying to hook the front wheel. Using a specialized ebike wall mounting system like GearHooks allows you to roll the bike up without lifting it, which is the safest method for heavy 2026 models.

Why are police cracking down on e-bikes?

Police Constable Mustafa Jameel at Metropolitan Police added: “Our joint operations with Hounslow Council last week resulted in several illegal e-bikes being taken off the streets. Many were fitted with unsafe or high-powered motors. These e-bikes break the law and endanger riders and the wider public." Always ensure your e-bike complies with local UK regulations before traveling in 2026.

Can you hang a bike on a plasterboard wall?

Wall mounts can be used on plasterboard (or drywall) with the help of anchors. In this case, it is better to find the wooden frame (studs) behind the wall and screw into these where possible to ensure the heavy duty bike wall mount can handle the weight of an e-bike safely. For very heavy bikes, fixing into solid masonry or timber is always recommended.

Should you hang a bike by the front or back tire?

Front or Rear Wheel? Both are fine, though most people use the front wheel for convenience when using an ebike storage hook. Just make sure your hook or hanger is padded so it doesn't scratch your rim. The GearHooks system features a protective rubber sleeve to keep your wheels pristine.
