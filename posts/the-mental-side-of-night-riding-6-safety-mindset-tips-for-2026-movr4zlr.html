Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to hang ladders in a garage?

Installing heavy-duty wall hooks or ladder racks allows you to securely hang your ladder. At GearHooks, we recommend using a rail-based system where ladder hooks for garage can be positioned exactly where you need them. What are common ladder hook problems?

Most problems come from incorrect hook placement, poor ridge contact, or incompatible hook designs that don't fit the ladder's rungs properly. Fixing these issues usually involves adjusting hook alignment, improving ladder stabilization, and inspecting hook wear before climbing. Using a CNC-machined clamp system like that found in GearHooks ensures the hook stays exactly where you set it on the rail.

How much weight can garage hooks hold?

Hooks with higher weight capacities, like the heavy duty ladder hooks for garage found in our BR series, can support up to 150kg when used on the rail. For lighter items, smaller single hooks with a 35 lbs capacity may suffice. It is essential to choose a rack that matches your specific storage needs, whether you are hanging a lightweight carbon road bike or a heavy 2026 mountain e-bike.

How to hang heavy items on a garage wall?

Mounting things on the garage wall can be as simple as screwing a basic hook into the wall, but this offers no flexibility. A more adaptable and flexible solution is to use a wall-mounted garage wall rack. These comprise a cold-rolled steel rail that is screwed to the wall with heavy-duty fixings, and then individual hooks fit to the rail. This allows you to store heavy bikes, ladders, and tools in one place without drilling dozens of holes.
