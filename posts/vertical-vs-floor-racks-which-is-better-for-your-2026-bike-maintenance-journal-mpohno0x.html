Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use home depot hooks for garage to hang a heavy e-bike?

While some heavy-duty hooks may work, most generic utility hooks are not rated for the 25kg+ weight of modern e-bikes. It is safer to use a dedicated e-bike wall rack with reinforced fixings and a steel rail to ensure the bike doesn't fall and damage itself or your garage.

Is vertical bike storage bad for hydraulic disc brakes?

Generally, no. Modern hydraulic systems are sealed. If your brakes feel spongy after hanging, it usually indicates air was already in the system, and the vertical position simply moved the bubble. Regular maintenance, tracked in your 2026 journal, will involve occasional bleeding to prevent this.
