Step-by-Step: How to Use Colored Hair Wax Like a Pro

Achieving a salon-quality finish at home is simple if you follow the right sequence. The goal is even distribution without clumping.

  1. Prep: Start with clean, slightly damp or dry hair. Using a water soluble hair color on dry hair usually yields more vibrant results.
  2. Scoop: Take a small amount of colored hair wax and rub it between your palms until it is warm and creamy.
  3. Apply: Work from the roots to the tips. Use your fingers to define sections. For a subtle look, use a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Style: Once the color is distributed, style as usual. The wax provides its own hold.
  5. Wash: When you're ready to remove it, simply rinse with warm water and your favorite shampoo.
