Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to hang ladders in a garage?

The best way to store a ladder is with a wall-mounted ladder rack or specialized garage hooks for ladders. These options keep ladders off the floor, protect them from damage, and save space in your garage or shed. By keeping the ladder horizontal and high on the wall, you prevent it from tipping over and damaging your bikes or car.

How do I choose the right ladder hooks?

To find the right ladder hook, contact your ladder's manufacturer to see if it has a hook designed to work with its ladders. If it doesn't offer any, review the ladder hook manufacturer's guidelines to see what types of ladders are best suited for its hook. Always ensure the hook is rated for the full weight of the ladder and any attached accessories.

How much weight can garage hooks hold?

Hooks with higher weight capacities, like the Heavy Duty Cradle Hook supporting up to 200 lbs, are ideal for heavy tools and equipment. For e-bikes, look for systems that can handle up to 300kg across the rail. A variety of weight capacities are available to accommodate different storage needs, so always check the product specifications before mounting.

What are the different types of ladder hooks?

Roof ladder hooks come in one of three types: flat steel, rubberized, and captain. Flat steel is the most common type for general storage. When selecting hooks for a garage, you might also see "J-hooks" or "Cradle hooks" designed specifically for ladder storage hooks ceiling or wall mounts to provide a secure rest for the rungs.
