Frequently Asked Questions

Can a bike wall mount indoor hold a heavy e-bike?

Yes, but you must choose a heavy-duty model like the GearHooks 3-bike mount. It is designed to handle the weight of motors and batteries, and you can even set it up so you roll the bike onto the hook without lifting it.

Will the hooks scratch my carbon rims?

Quality mounts use a soft rubber or PVC coating on the hooks. This protects the rim from scratches and provides grip to prevent the bike from shifting while hanging.

Is a vertical mount better than a horizontal one?

Vertical mounts are generally superior for small garages because they allow you to store more bikes in less width. Horizontal mounts are often better for displaying a single bike as a piece of art.

How much wall space do I need for 3 bikes?

With a vertical rail system, you can store three bikes in a space just 1 meter wide. This is achieved by staggering the heights or slightly angling the handlebars.

Can I install this on a plasterboard wall?

Yes, provided you fix the rail into the wooden studs behind the plasterboard. Alternatively, use heavy-duty toggle bolts, though fixing into studs is always recommended for heavy bikes.

What is the difference between a DIY mount and a professional rail?

A DIY solution often involves separate hooks that require many holes in your wall. A professional rail system like GearHooks only requires three holes and allows you to adjust the hooks later without more drilling.
