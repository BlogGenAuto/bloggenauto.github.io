Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Gen Z ploughing their headphones in?

The reasons are a mix of nostalgia, aesthetic, and a quiet rejection of tech dependency. Gen Z listeners are increasingly drawn to the simplicity of wired designs that give them a feeling of the Nintendo 64 or the Game Boy era. This movement highlights a desire for tangible connections and a "lo-fi" lifestyle in an increasingly wireless world.

What earbuds are trending?

In 2026, the trendiest earbuds are those featuring "Open-Ear" designs and advanced spatial audio capabilities. Users are looking for earbuds that don't fully block the ear canal, allowing for better situational awareness while maintaining high-fidelity sound. Transparent casings and vibrant, non-traditional colors like neon green and metallic silver are also highly popular.

What headphones are Gen Z wearing?

For years, wireless earbuds symbolized convenience and status. But Gen Z doesn't always follow trends blindly. Instead, they question value, performance, and authenticity. Today, Gen Z wearing wired headphones is less about nostalgia and more about smart, intentional choices, often opting for chunky over-ear models that double as a fashion accessory during their daily commute.

What are the latest trends in wireless headphones?

The latest trends focus heavily on sustainability and AI integration. Many new models for 2026 use recycled plastics and lab-grown leathers. Furthermore, AI-enhanced noise cancellation that can distinguish between annoying background noise and an important announcement (like a flight number at an airport) is becoming a standard feature in premium models.
