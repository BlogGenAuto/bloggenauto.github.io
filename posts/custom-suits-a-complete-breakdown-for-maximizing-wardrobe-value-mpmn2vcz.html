Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to buy handmade suits online without a fitting?

Yes, in 2026, AI-powered measurement systems capture over 20 body points from photos, ensuring a fit that is often more accurate than a manual tape measure. Brands like WIAI have perfected this technology to provide a satisfaction guarantee on fit.

What makes a navy blue wool suit the most versatile?

Navy blue works with almost any skin tone and pairs perfectly with both black and brown accessories. Its ability to look formal as a full set or casual when deconstructed makes it the #1 recommendation for a modular wardrobe.

How do I care for a custom wool business suit?

Avoid frequent dry cleaning, which can damage wool fibers. Instead, use a garment steamer to remove wrinkles and a horsehair brush to remove surface dust. Hang it on a wide cedar hanger to maintain the shoulder shape.

How long does it take to receive a custom suit?

Most premium handmade suits online are delivered within 3-4 weeks. This includes the time for precision cutting, hand-stitching the canvas, and quality control checks before shipping to your door.

Can I customize the lining with my own images?

Many high-end services now offer a custom suit with pictures inside where you can upload digital designs or photos that are then printed onto the silk or cupro lining for a truly bespoke experience.

Is wool suitable for all seasons in 2026?

Yes, specifically 250g weight wool (like our Navy Blue set) is considered "all-season." It provides warmth in winter while the natural breathability of the fibers keeps you cool during summer months.
