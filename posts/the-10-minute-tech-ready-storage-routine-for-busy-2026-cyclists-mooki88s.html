FAQ

Will these garage wall hooks support a heavy e-bike?

The GearHooks BRZ1 is specifically designed for road bikes and lighter hybrids up to 20kg. While the steel construction is incredibly strong, we recommend checking the weight of your e-bike first. For heavier bikes, we offer specialized vertical solutions that can accommodate up to 25kg or more per hook.

Can I install the GearRail on a plasterboard wall?

Yes, the system includes hex head wall bolts suitable for wood or plasterboard battens, as well as expanding nylon fixings for brick, block, or concrete. For plasterboard, we always recommend securing the rail into the timber studs for maximum stability and safety.

How do I make my bike sit level if the top tube is angled?

This is a key advantage of the GearHooks system. Because the two bike hooks can be positioned independently on the 1-meter rail, you can set one hook slightly higher than the other to perfectly compensate for a sloping top tube, keeping your bike level for display.

Are the hooks safe for high-end carbon frames?

Absolutely. Each hook is covered in a soft rubber sleeve that cushions the frame and prevents any metal-to-carbon contact. This ensures your paintwork and structural integrity remain pristine, even with daily use.

Is it difficult to fold the hooks when they aren't in use?

Not at all. The design features a simple folding mechanism that allows the brackets to sit flush against the wall. This is a favorite feature for garage owners who need to park a car in the same space where they store their bikes.
