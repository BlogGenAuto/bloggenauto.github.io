How do I use hair cream pomade for the best results?

Apply to slightly damp hair for a sleeker finish or dry hair for a matte, natural look. Start with a small amount, rub it until clear in your hands, and work it through from the roots to the tips to ensure even distribution.

Is water-soluble pomade better for my hair?

Yes, because it prevents the buildup that can clog pores and weigh down hair. It allows your hair to stay healthy and clean without the need for aggressive sulfate-based shampoos every day.
