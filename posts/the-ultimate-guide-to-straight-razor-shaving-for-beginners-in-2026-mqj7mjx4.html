The 30-Degree Rule

The most common mistake is the angle of the blade. Hold the razor at roughly a 30-degree angle to the skin. Too steep, and you'll cut yourself; too flat, and you'll pull the hair. Use short, light strokes and let the weight of the razor do the work.
