FAQ

What is a clay pomade?

A clay pomade is a hair styling product that uses natural clays like kaolin to provide texture, volume, and a matte finish without the greasy shine typical of other styling products.

Why do celebrities prefer clay pomades?

Celebrities choose clay pomades for their ability to create a natural look with a strong hold, perfect for red carpet events and everyday appearances.

Are clay pomades suitable for all hair types?

Yes, clay pomades are versatile and can be used on various hair types, including thick, thin, curly, or straight hair, making them a staple in many celebrities' grooming kits.

How do I apply a clay pomade?

For best results, apply a small amount to damp hair, working it through evenly from roots to tips. Style as desired using your fingers or a comb.
