Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use my pocket comb during the day?

For most hair types in 2026, 2-3 quick touch-ups are sufficient to maintain a polished look. Over-combing can sometimes lead to flattened volume, so focus on using it when you transition from one environment to another, such as leaving your car to enter an office.

Does an anti-static comb really work on all hair types?

Yes, while the effects are most noticeable on fine or dry hair, the material helps reduce friction for thick and curly hair as well. It ensures that the hair cuticle remains flat, which is essential for shine across all textures.

Can I use a pocket comb on a wet beard?

It is generally better to comb your beard when it is damp rather than soaking wet. Hair is at its weakest when saturated. Using a men's comb on a damp beard with a little beard oil will provide the smoothest glide and best styling results.

How do I clean my oil-infused pocket comb?

Simply rinse it with lukewarm water and a mild soap once a week to remove any buildup of styling products. Avoid harsh chemicals that might strip the beneficial oil infusion from the material.

Is a pocket comb better than a folding comb?

Pocket combs are often preferred for their slim profile and lack of moving parts, which makes them more durable for daily carry in a back pocket. They are less likely to break at the hinge, which is a common fail point for folding models.
