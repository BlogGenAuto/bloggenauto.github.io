Frequently Asked Questions

Are storage hooks garage systems safe for carbon fiber frames?

Yes, provided the hooks are designed with soft rubber sleeves. Professional systems like GearHooks use protective coatings to ensure the frame is never in direct contact with metal, preventing scratches or structural stress. In 2026, this is standard for high-end storage.

Can I use these hooks for heavy e-bikes?

Most horizontal hooks are rated for bikes up to 20kg. If your e-bike is heavier, you may need to look at vertical storage solutions or heavy-duty industrial rails. Always check the load rating of your wall fixings before mounting an e-bike.

How do I mount a bike rack on a brick wall?

Use expanding nylon fixings and hex head wall bolts. These provide the necessary grip in masonry to support both the weight of the bike and the leverage applied when using the rack as a workstand. Most premium UK manufactured systems include these fixings as standard.

Do horizontal bike hooks work with sloping top tubes?

Yes. By using a modular rail system, you can mount one hook higher than the other. This allows you to level the bike regardless of the frame geometry, ensuring it looks perfect on your wall.

Why should I avoid cheap storage hooks toolstation alternatives?

Cheap alternatives often use thin metal or poor-quality plastic that can bend over time. A professional 2026 setup requires cold-rolled steel and a lifetime warranty to ensure your bike never falls unexpectedly.
