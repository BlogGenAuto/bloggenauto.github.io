Frequently Asked Questions

How to hang stuff on garage walls?

To hang stuff effectively on garage walls, you should identify the wall material (brick, concrete, or timber studs) and use appropriate fixings. Utilizing a modular system like garage hanging hooks allows you to drill fewer holes while supporting heavier loads, ensuring your bikes and gear stay secure for years to come.

How much weight can garage hooks hold?

The weight capacity depends on the quality of the hook and the wall fixings. Heavy-duty hooks like the GearHooks BZ1 are designed to hold up to 20kg per bike, which easily accommodates most road, city, and gravel bikes. Always ensure you are using expanding nylon fixings for masonry or hex head bolts for timber to maximize strength.

What are the different types of garage hooks?

There are several types of hooks, including vertical hangers (where the bike hangs by the wheel), horizontal bike storage brackets (where the bike sits on the frame), and multi-purpose utility hooks. Folding bike hooks are particularly popular in 2026 for their ability to fold flat against the wall when not in use.

Are self-adhesive hooks any good?

For heavy equipment like bicycles, self-adhesive hooks are not recommended as they lack the mechanical strength to support 10-20kg safely. For reliable home bike organization, always opt for screw-in steel bike wall mounts that provide a permanent, secure connection to the building's structure.
