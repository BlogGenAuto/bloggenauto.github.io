FAQ: Common Questions About Online Custom Suits

Are online custom suits as good as traditional bespoke?

While traditional bespoke involves multiple hand-measured fittings, modern online custom suits use AI to achieve 95-99% accuracy. For most grooms, the difference in fit is negligible, while the cost savings are significant.

What is half-canvas construction?

Half-canvas construction uses a horsehair/wool layer between the suit fabric and the lining from the shoulder to the chest. This helps the jacket maintain its shape and provides a more natural drape than fused jackets.

Is light grey appropriate for a formal evening wedding?

Yes, especially in 2026. While black is traditional, the light grey satin texture provides a modern, sophisticated alternative that works for both daytime and evening events.

How long does the online tailoring process take?

Typically, from measurement to delivery, the process takes 3-5 weeks. We always recommend ordering at least 8 weeks before your event to allow for minor local alterations if desired.

Can I customize the lapels and buttons?

Absolutely. Most online custom suits platforms allow you to choose lapel style (notch or peak), button configuration, and even interior lining colors to match your wedding theme.
