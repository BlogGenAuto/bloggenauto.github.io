FAQs

What makes water-based pomades different from oil-based ones?

Water-based pomades provide a lighter, more flexible hold and are easier to wash out, reducing the risk of buildup and greasiness.

How does sodium hyaluronate benefit hair?

Sodium hyaluronate deeply moisturizes the hair and scalp, preventing dryness and improving hair texture and shine.

Can water-based pomades work for all hair types?

Yes, they are designed to be versatile, offering suitable hold and texture for curly, straight, and wavy hair alike.

How often should I use a pomade?

This depends on your styling needs, but generally, pomades can be used daily without causing damage, provided they are washed out regularly.

What is the best way to apply pomade?

Apply a small amount to damp hair, distribute evenly with fingers or a comb, and style as desired. Adjust the amount based on hair thickness and length.
