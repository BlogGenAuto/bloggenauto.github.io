Yes, the GearHooks 4 bike wall rack is designed to handle heavy loads. For e-bikes, we recommend the 'NoLift' installation where the rear wheel rests on the floor, allowing the rail to support the weight without the need for heavy lifting.

Is this better than buying individual garage hooks home depot?

Absolutely. Individual garage hooks home depot require more drilling and offer less stability. A single 1-metre rail distributes weight more effectively and allows you to adjust the spacing between bikes as your collection grows.

Yes, the hooks are designed to accommodate wheel sizes from 20" up to 29" and tyre widths up to 3 inches, making it perfect for modern mountain bike storage in 2026.

How do I secure my cycling safety gear to the rack?

Every bike hook features a security loop at the top. You can thread a cable lock through the loop, your bike frame, and even your helmet straps to create a secure, unified storage system.

Yes, the system is fully modular. You can buy extra hooks for helmets, backpacks, or tools and slide them onto the existing rail using just an Allen key.
